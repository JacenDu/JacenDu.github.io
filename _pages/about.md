Hello! I am Jiachen Du, a Ph.D. Candidate in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at **Tsinghua University**. I am currently conducting my research at the [Future Laboratory](https://thfl.tsinghua.edu.cn/) , advised by Prof. Jeung Jihong and co-advised by Prof. Haipeng Mi.

I had the privilege of being a Postgraduate Visiting Student at the HCI Group at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojuan Ma. Prior to my Ph.D. journey, I received my Master's degree in Data Science from **The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)**.

My research lies at the intersection of human-centered AI and data science. I am particularly interested in:
* **Personal Informatics:** Designing systems that help individuals collect and reflect on personal data.
* **Computational Neuroscience:** Applying computational models to understand brain function and behavior.
* **Health Informatics:** Developing data-driven technologies to support health monitoring and well-being.
