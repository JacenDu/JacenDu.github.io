This study introduces TaskWise, an AI-driven to-do list management tool enhancing cognitive performance through interactive detail enrichment. TaskWise addresses the challenge of balancing simplified recording with sufficient initiation context by leveraging Large Language Models for real-time task analysis and proactive detail prompting, supporting effective task initiation and preventing key information omission. A seven-day, six-participant user study showed TaskWise's AI detail inference significantly improved users' task understanding, preparedness, and sense of control. AI-generated prompts served as an effective "cognitive scaffold," aiding memory recall and planning. Users also exhibited significant agency, strategically adopting AI-suggested details based on task context. These findings demonstrate AI's potential to assist task definition and enhance management experience, offering insights for designing future intelligent systems that better support cognitive processes and empower user agency.
