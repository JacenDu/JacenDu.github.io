This project presents HopeFix, an AI-powered augmented reality (AR) toy repair system designed to foster children’s experience of hope through hands-on activities. Grounded in Hope Theory, HopeFix scaffolds goal setting, pathways thinking, and agency by combining AR instructions with inquiry-based AI voice prompts. Informed by formative research with children, parents, and repair experts, we developed a system demo and a low-fidelity interactive prototype. We also conducted a preliminary user study with seven parent–child dyads to explore the system’s usability and its potential to activate hope-related thinking.
